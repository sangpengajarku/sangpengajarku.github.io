    1 potato diced

    1 stalk lemongrass crushed

    Salt , sugar , cooking oil , and enough water


HOW TO MAKE 

    Heat a little oil and saute onion and garlic until fragrant .
    Insert meat chicken , carrots , potatoes , ginger , lemongrass , scallions , pepper , sugar , salt , and add enough water to soak all of the contents of the soup and cook until everything is cooked and the meat is tender .
    Enter celery , lift and serve while warm chicken soup with fried onions sprinkled on top , is perfect eaten with white rice and sauce / ketchup .

2. resep sup ayam menggunakan bahasa inggris?


1. Bring chicken, water, and 1 tablespoon salt to a boil in a large stockpot. Skim foam. Add onions, celery, and garlic. Reduce heat. Simmer, partially covered, for 30 minutes.

2. Remove breast, and set aside. Add carrots. Simmer, partially covered, for 40 minutes.

3. Remove remaining chicken; discard back and wings. Let cool slightly. Remove meat from bones, and cut into bite-size pieces.

4. Stir in desired amount of chicken; reserve the rest for another use. Skim fat. Season with salt.emang pake b.inggris, iyakan

6. Resep sup ayam yang bahasanya pake bahasa inggris dan terjemahanya


ENGLISH

Ingredients:

350 gr chicken (cut into pieces)
1 medium-size carrot (cut into pieces), 2 fruit potatoes (diced)
1 stalk scallion pods (finely sliced)
fried onions for topping to taste
1 red tomato (cut into pieces)
5 thread the nut string beans (cut each 3 cm)
2 pieces of cabbage (cut into pieces)
salt to taste
granulated sugar to taste
flavor enhancer to taste
water sparingly
oil for sauteing to taste
Spice paste:
5 grains of red onion
2 cloves garlic
2 roasted hazelnut grains
1/2 teaspoon pepper powder

The Process Of Making Chicken Soup:

Chicken cut into pieces that are already washed with water until it is completely clean, drain
Saute seasoning which is mashed with a little oil until fragrant
pour the water into the sauteed seasoning to taste, wait until boiling
Enter the chicken pieces into the boiled marinade, let stand until cooked and tender
put all the vegetables already cut into the chicken stew
Add sugar, salt and flavor enhancer, stirring until well blended and matured
turn off the heat and put pieces of tomato and slices scallion pods
soup chicken teapot
