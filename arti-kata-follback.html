Dan dia akan memberikan komentar follback pada komentar foto atau memberikan dm yang berisikan follbackDm : Dm ialah merupakan kepanjangan dari direct message. - To be included in someone's internal contact/ list , you need to 'follow' in Instagram. Same as 'add as a friend' on Facebook. 
- To return the 'follow' from other party, you click 'follow back' so that you're both in each other's internal contact list. 
 - To send a private message to one of your friends or in Instagram it's called 'followers', you click 'DM' or 'direct message'.
