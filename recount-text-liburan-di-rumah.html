Jawaban:

My Worst Holiday

Last semester break was my worst holiday I have ever passed. It was two weeks long and I just stayed at home. I just did the same activities routinely, over and over again.

I woke up and had a breakfast in the morning. Then I fed my Persian cat and played with it. In the afternoon I had lunch and then took a nap on the couch. In the evening, I dined and watched my favorite TV shows. There was nothing special on my daily activities during the last holiday. So, I was sure that it was the worst holiday I’ve ever had.

Liburan Terburukku

Liburan semester yang lalu merupakan liburan terburukku yang pernah aku alami. Liburan tersebut selama dua minggu dan aku hanya tinggal di rumah saja. Aku hanya melakukan aktifitas yang sama, lagi dan lagi.
