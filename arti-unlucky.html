I. Change into passive Form !

a. Does he ever catch anything ?

Passive: Is anything ever caught by him?

b. Some unlucky men often catch rubbish or old boots

Passive: Rubbish or old boots are often caught by some unlucky men.

c. He usually goes back home with an empty bag.

Passive: An empty bag is what he usually gone back home with.

d. They don’t realize one important thing

Passive: One important thing is not realized by them.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan menggunakan bentuk kalimat Passive Voice.

Rumus Passive Voice:

1. Passive Voice [Simple Present Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + Verb3

2. Passive Voice [Present Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (is/am/are) + being + Verb3

3. Passive Voice [Present Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + have/has + been + Verb3

4. Passive Voice [Simple Past Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Verb 3

5. Passive Voice [Past Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + to be (was – were) + Being + Verb3

6. Passive Voice [Past Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + had + been + Verb3

7. Passive Voice [Simple Future Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will + be + Verb3

8. Passive Voice [Future Continuous Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will be + being + Verb3

9. Passive Voice [Future Perfect Tense]

-Pattern = Subject + will have + been + Verb3

24. Unlucky Teacher or Lebai Malang" Questions:. What is the purpose of writing the text above? Answer:. What is the text about? Answer:. What was Lebai Malang's problem? Answer: Why did Lebai return his boat to the southern village? Answer: What does the story tell us about? Answer:​.


1.  Purpose of writing the text "Unlucky Teacher or Lebai Malang" is to entertain and amuse the readers.

2. The text is about Lebai Malang.

3. Lebai Malang's problem was that he always got unlucky.

4. Lebai return his boat to the southern village because he saw many people going there then he thought that he would not get any food.

5. The story tell us about we can't be so greedy and calculating in life.

Pembahasan

Text "Unlucky Teacher or Lebai Malang" termasuk dari jenis teks narrative. Narrative adalah jenis teks berupa cerita yang menceritakan sesuatu atau kejadian yang imajiner tetapi terkadang bias juga factual dalam bentuk lisan maupun tulisan. Terdapat berbagai jenis cerita narrative diantaranya fairytale, folklore, fable, dan lain-lain. Teks narrative bertujuan untuk menghibur pembaca atau pendengarnya. Selain itu tujuan dari text narrative untuk mendidik atau meneladani dngan nilai-nilai moral yang terkandung dalam cerita tersebut, sehingga dengan memahami cerita text narrative peserta didik dapat trhibur dan terbentuk karakter moralnya, karena dalam text narrative mengandung amanat yang sarat dengan nilai.
