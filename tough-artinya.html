2. Apa perbedaan arti though, tough dan through?


Though : Meskipun/Walaupun/Padahal

Contoh kalimat :

"Though I hate your games, I'll still be friends with you."

Tough : Sulit/Kuat

Contoh kalimat :

"Working in pizza planet is so very tough, Kelly."

"Wow, this guy is tough"

Through : Melalui/Melewati

Contoh kalimat :

"He went to the supermarket through the short passage way, he's so smart."

"Finally, I've gone through this for forever!"

semoga mengerti

though:meskipun

tough:sulit

through:melalui

5. Penggunaan kata "Tough" dan "difficult" itu bagaimana y?


Jawaban:

Tough adalah kata sifat yang berarti “sulit” atau “sukar”. Hal yang perlu diperhatikan adalah karena tough kata sifat biasanya terletak sebelum kata benda atau terletak setelah very atau a

Difficult bermakna sulit, sukar, dan problematis. Kesulitan yang dirujuk difficult menunjukkan sesuatu yang rumit dan problematis.

Penjelasan:

Contoh

Tough:

The government will make a tough decisions.

(pemerintah akan membuat sebuah keputusan yang sulit.

Difficult:

The last exam is the most difficult exam for Clara.

(Ujian terakhir adalah ujian paling sulit bagi Clara)

8. apakah perbedaan dari tough, though, thought, through? dan apa maksudnya?


tough= sulit , though = meskipun ,thought = pikir , through =melaluiTough = Sulit, kuat 
Contoh : He's a tough man

Though = Meskipun
Contoh : The alarm woke me up, though it wasn't very loud

Thought = Pemikiran (noun), berpikir (verb 2)
Contoh : I thought that I was stronger

Through = Melalui
Contoh : We went through the tunnel
