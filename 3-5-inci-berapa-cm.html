3 inci = 7,5 × 10

3 inci = 75 mm

No. 2

10 inci = 25 cm

10 inci = (10 × 2,5) × 1/100

10 inci = 25 × 100

10 inci = 25/100

10 inci = 0,25 m

No. 3

1 m = 100/2,5

1 m = 40 inci
