3. cara buat omelet gimana ?


materials to make omelets. Tools and materials used include:
1. instant noodles
2. 2 (two) eggs
3. place
4. frying pan
5. dishes
6. place to drain
7. water
8. spatula
9. spoon
Once the tools and materials are ready, put water in place. Then place the place on the stove. Turn on the stove and wait for the water to boil. After boiling, put instant noodles into it. Wait for instant noodles to mature.
Once cooked then lift the instant noodles and drain in place to drain. Then prepare 2 eggs. Break the egg into place. Combine the egg with cooked instant noodles, instant noodle spice, sugar, and salt to taste. Stir the material with a spoon until the dough is evenly distributed.
Then, prepare the frying pan. Place the frying pan on the stove. Add the oil to the frying pan. After that turn on the stove. Wait until the oil in the frying pan.
After the hot oil, put the pre-prepared dough into the pan. Flatten and wait until cooked. When fried in the dough should always be back and forth. Once cooked, drain the omelete into place to drain. Afterwards serve to the plate and ready to eat.
Above are the steps of making omelete. Omelete can be made anytime and anywhere such as, for breakfast, for lunch to school, supplies to the office, and so forth because making omelete very easy and simple. Good luck. Thank

Egg omelette recipe

Material:

- 5 eggs, shake off

- 5 pieces of bacon, cut into small squares

- 1 onion, roughly chopped

- 75 grams tinned button mushrooms, thinly sliced

- 50 grams of pipil corn

- 1/2 red pepper, cut into small squares

- 1/2 green pepper, cut into small squares

- 50 grams grated cheddar cheese

- 1/2 tablespoon salt

- 1/4 teaspoon ground peppercorns

- 1/2 teaspoon English soy sauce

- 50 ml of thick cream (can change yogurt)

- 1 tablespoon cooking oil to sauté

How to make an egg omelette:

1. Mix eggs, bacon, mushrooms, corn, cheese, salt, pepper, English soy sauce and thick cream.

2. Sauté the onion and peppers. Pour in the egg mixture. Leave it on for a while until there are parts that begin to mature. Stir so that the egg liquid flows down.

3. Roll the shape of the omelette while pressed on the edge of the pan until cooked.
