✔ Kelas. = 7 SMP
✔ Materi = Aritmatika Sosial
✔ Kata kunci = Untung

Penyelesaian :
➡➡➡➡➡➡➡➡➡➡➡➡

Untung
= Harga Jual - Harga Beli
= Rp 1.750.000 - Rp 1.250.000
= Rp 500.000

Persentase Keuntungan
= Untung/Harga Beli x 100%
= Rp 500.000/Rp 1.250.000 x 100%
= 50/125 x 100%
= 2/5 x 100%
= 2 x 20%
= 40%
