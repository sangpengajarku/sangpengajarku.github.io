15. berusaha bahasa Inggris


Berusaha dalam bahasa Inggris memiliki banyak arti, berikut ini saya paparkan beserta contohnya di dalam sebuah kalimat

Pembahasan

try  :

he tried to regain his breath (dia berusaha memulihkan nafasnya)you have to try it once again (kamu harus berusaha sekali lagi)

essay  :

she essays to smile after taking a hard time (dia berusaha tersenyum kembali setelah mengalami masa berat)a misjudge essay (usaha yang salah dipahami)

undertake  :

She refused to undertake any enterprise without his involvement.  (Dia menolak untuk melakukan usaha apa pun tanpa keterlibatannya.)To undertake the duties and responsibilities of a platoon sergeant, you are expected to be at the top of your game.  (Untuk mengusahakan tugas dan tanggung jawab sersan peleton, Anda diharapkan berada di puncak permainan Anda)I undertake that that note will be provided and circulated tomorrow morning.  (Saya berusaha agar surat itu akan diberikan dan diedarkan besok pagi.)He must undertake great enterprises that allow him to display his abilities.  (Dia harus melakukan usaha besar yang memungkinkan dia untuk menunjukkan kemampuannya.)

attempt :

she attempted to come back to her last company (dia telah berusaha untuk kembali pada perusahaan lamanya)I don't want to live like this anymore, I want make attempt (aku tidak ingin hidup seperti ini lagi, aku ingin berusaha)

make efforts  :

she made effort to come back to her last company (dia telah berusaha untuk kembali pada perusahaan lamanya)I don't want to live like this anymore, I want make efforts (aku tidak ingin hidup seperti ini lagi, aku ingin berusaha)

exert  :

he needs to exert himself to try to find an answer (dia harus berusaha untuk mendapatkan jawabannya)if you want get an answer, exert yourself (jika kamu ingin mendapatkan jawabannya, berusahalah)

labor  :

they labored from dawn to dusk in two shifts (mereka berusaha / bekerja dari subuh hingga maghrib dalam dua shift)don't labor yourself for what is unnecessary (jangan usahakan / paksa dirimu untuk sesuatu yang tidak penting)

coax  :

the trainees were coaxed into doing hard (para siswa didik diusahakan untuk bekerja keras)they coaxed from dawn to dusk in two shifts (mereka berusaha / bekerja dari subuh hingga maghrib dalam dua shift)don't coax yourself for what is unnecessary (jangan usahakan / paksa dirimu untuk sesuatu yang tidak penting)

solicit  :

he called a meeting to solicit their views (dia membuat perkumpulan untuk berusaha agar mendapatkan pandangan mereka)

take pains  :

If you want something, you have to take pains to get it (jika kamu ingin sesuatu, kamu harus berusaha untuk mendapatkannya)

make a try  :

he made a try to regain his breath (dia berusaha memulihkan nafasnya)you have to make a try it once again (kamu harus berusaha sekali lagi)

Demikianlah kata kata bahasa Inggris yang memiliki arti berusaha, namun sebenarnya masih ada lagi kata kata lainnya yang belum terjabarkan di sini, jika kita mau mencarinya di dalam kamus, maka kita akan mendapati banyak sekali kata kata yang berarti berusaha. 28. Saya kerja di perusahaan swasta bahasa inggrisnya apa??


PART = INTERPRETATION - PRESENT PERFECT CONTINUOUS-PRESENT PERFECT

-------------

Answer :

1. I have been working in the private company. ( if it is still going on (jika masih berlanjut sampai saat ini )

2. I have worked in the private company . ( If it is related to the experience of working place ( jika berkaitan dengan pengalaman tempat kerja )

EXPLANATION :

Perusahaan swasta in general, we call Into  three meanings such as :

1. The private company

2. Privately held company

3. Privately owned company

The word “privately held” is an adjective , it is group of phrases that modifies  its noun “company.” It is called as private , it means (that ) it doesn’t belong to the state’s property or publicly traded  but it belongs to small number of investors, or group of founders who have the close relationship like family.

Now, return to the translation! “Saya kerja di perusahaan swasta.” In general, if you neglect  English grammar  or you click on the translation engine, it will be translated into “I work in the private company.” Actually, it is totally wrong.

If we talk about the situation, It should be written “ I have been working in the private company.” We have to use the present perfect continuous tense because it is still going on until now. It is related to the continuity, which the activity began in  the past and still continue to the present (now).

Meanwhile, we write it by using the present perfect,  “ i have worked in the private company ” means that the activity just finished recently. It  means that " i am not working in the private company now"  but I just resigned. The use of present perfect tells about the experience of working place or reflects the past activity which has the relationship to the present
