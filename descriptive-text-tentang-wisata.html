Sedudo Waterfall is a waterfall and tourist attraction located in Ngliman Village, Sawahan District, Nganjuk Regency, East Java. The distance is about 30 km south of the capital city of Nganjuk district. Located at an altitude of 1,438 meters above sea level, the height of this waterfall is about 105 meters. This tourist spot has quite good facilities, and transportation routes that are easily accessible.

Local people still believe that this waterfall has supernatural powers. This natural tourist location is visited by many people in the month of Sura (Javanese calendar). It is said that a myth that has existed since the Majapahit era, in that month is believed to bring the blessing of youth for those who bathe in the waterfall.

Every Javanese New Year, the Sedudo waterfall is used for ritual ceremonies, namely bathing the statue in the Parna Prahista ceremony, which then splashes the rest of the water for the family to get the blessing of safety and youth. Until now, the Nganjuk Regency Government has routinely held the Mandi Sedudo ritual every 1st Suro.

A tradition that is no less important is Jamasan Pusaka, which is usually performed at the Ngliman Village office. Jamasan Pusaka is carried out by washing heirlooms such as kris, spears and other heirlooms from the Majapahit Kingdom.

The Eiffel Tower

Penjelasan:

The Eiffel Tower is an iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris. Built in 1889, it has become both a global icon of France and one of the most recognizable structures in the world. The tower is the tallest building in Paris and the most-visited paid monument in the world; millions of people ascend it every year.

Named for its designer, engineer Gustave Eiffel, the tower was built as the entrance arch to the 1889 World's Fair.The tower stands 324 metres (1,063 ft) tall, about the same height as an 81-story building. Upon its completion, it surpassed the Washington Monument to assume the title of tallest man-made structure in the world, a title it held for 41 years, until the Chrysler Building in New York City was built in 1930; however, due to the addition in 1957 of the antenna, the tower is now taller than the Chrysler Building. Not including broadcast antennas, it is the second-tallest structure in France after the 2004 Millau Viaduct.

The tower has three levels for visitors. Tickets can be purchased to ascend, by stairs or lift, to the first and second levels. The walk to the first level is over 300 steps, as is the walk from the first to the second level. The third and highest level is accessible only by elevator. Both the first and second levels feature restaurants.

The tower has become the most prominent symbol of both Paris and France, often in the establishing shot of films set in the city.

21. buatlah sebuah text mengenai descriptive text tentang tempat wisata benteng fort roterdam and bira beach


Benteng Fort Rotterdam

Fort Rotterdam or Fort Ujung Pandang (Jum Pandang) is a heritage fortress of the Kingdom of Gowa-Tallo. The location of this fort is on the edge of the west coast of Makassar City, South Sulawesi.

The fort was built in 1545 by the 9th Gowa King named I Manrigau Daeng Bonto Karaeng Lakiung Tumapa'risi 'kallonna. Initially the fort was made of clay, but during the reign of the 14th Gowa King Sultan Alauddin the construction of the fort was replaced with padas sourced from the Karst Mountains in the Maros area. Ujung Pandang Fortress is shaped like a turtle who wants to crawl down into the sea. In terms of its shape it is very clear the philosophy of the Kingdom of Gowa, that turtles can live on land and at sea. So even with the Kingdom of Gowa that triumphed on land and in the ocean.

The original name of this fort was the Ujung Pandang Fortress, also the Gowa-Makassarese people called this fort Panyyua Fortress which was the headquarters of the frog troops of the Kingdom of Gowa. The kingdom of Gowa-Tallo finally signed the Bungayya agreement, which one of the articles required the Kingdom of Gowa to surrender this fort to the Dutch. When the Dutch occupied this fort, the name Benteng Ujung Pandang was changed to Fort Rotterdam. Cornelis Speelman deliberately chose the name Fort Rotterdam to commemorate his birthplace in the Netherlands. The fort was later used by the Dutch as a center for spice shelters in eastern Indonesia.

At the Fort Ujung Pandang complex, nowadays, there is the La Galigo Museum in which there are many references to the history of the greatness of Makassar (Gowa-Tallo) and other areas in South Sulawesi. Most of the fort buildings are still intact and become one of the attractions in the city of Makassar.

Pembahasan

Descriptive text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu (manusia, hewan, tumbuhan dan benda) secara khusus atau spesifik.

Goal: to describe somethong or someone specifically.

Generic Structure dari Descriptive text adalah:

1. Identification: Berisi tentang penjelasan singkat dari apa yang akan di deskripsikan.

2. Description: Mendeskripsikan benda, hean, tumbuhan dan manusia secara spesifik atau khusus.

Language features dari Descriptive text adalah:

1. Menggunakan simple present tense

2. Menggunakan kata yang spesifik atau tidak terlalu umum

3. Menggunakan adjective untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu

CANDI BOROBUDUR

Borobudur is well-known all over the world. Its construction is influenced by the Gupta architecture of India. The temple is constructed on a hill 46 meter high and consists of eight steps like stone terrace. The first five terraces are square and surrounded by walls adorned with Buddhist sculpture in bas-relief. The upper three are circular. Each of them is with a circle of bell shape-stupa. The entire upper structure is crowned by a large stupa at the center of the top circle. The way to the summit extends through some 4.8 km of passage and stairways.

The design of Borobudur symbolizes the conception of universe in Buddhist cosmology. It is believed that the universe is divided into three spiritual spheres, kamadhatu, rupadhatu, and arupadhatu. The first sphere, kamadhatu, represents respectively the sphere of desires where we are bound to our desires; the second sphere, rupadhatu, represents forms where we abandon our desires but are still bound to name and form; and the last sphere, arupadhatu, represents formlessness where there is no longer either name or form. Borobudur temple which is rededicated as an Indonesian monument in 1983 is a valuable treasure for Indonesian people. With its magnificent size and architecture, no wonder that Borobudur Temple includes 7 wonders of the world.

Candi Borobudur merupakan sebuah candi Budha. Candi Borobudur dibangun pada abad ke-9 di bawah dinasti Sailendra dari kerajaan Mataram Kuno. Candi Borobudur terletak di Magelang, Jawa Tengah, Indonesia.

Candi Borobudur terkenal di seluruh dunia. Pembangunannya dipengaruhi oleh arsitektur Gupta dari India. Candi tersebut dibangun di atas bukit dengan tinggi 46 meter yang terdiri dari 8 tingkat seperti teras batu. Lima tingkat pertama berbentuk persegi dengan dikelilingi oleh dinding-dinding berhias kan ukiran dengan patung Budah di relief. Tiga tingkat di atasnya berbentuk lingkaran. Di setiap tingkatan itu terdapat stupa berbentuk lonceng. Di bagian paling atas seluruhnya dimahkotai oleh stupa besar di bagian tengah pada lingkaran atas. Jalan menuju puncak candi ditempuh dengan melalui jalan dan tangga sepanjang 4.8 km.

Desain Borobudur yang melambangkan struktur alam semesta dalam kosmologi budha. Hal ini diyakini bahwa alam semesta dibagi menjadi tiga bidang spiritual, Kamadhatu, Rupadhatu, dan Arupadhatu.
