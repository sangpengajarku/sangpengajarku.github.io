(expressing lack of necessity)

(expressing ability)

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal.

1. Modal Verb "can not" is expressing inability.

2. Is she able to merupakan Phrasal Modal Verb yang sesuai karena jawabannya menggunakan Tobe "is".

3. Modal Verb "must not" is expressing prohibition, alternatifnya "can not"

4. Modal Verb "may" is expressing possibility.

5. Modal Verb "could" is expressing ability.

6. Modal Verb "should" is giving advice.

7. Phrasal Modal Verb "had to" is expressing necessity.

8. Modal Verb "must" is expressing specific obligation.

9. Phrasal Modal Verb in Negative Form "don't have to" is expressing lack of necessity.

10. Phrasal Modal Verb "be able to" is expressing ability.
