15. Jika Andi memiliki tabungan Valas US$ 5,000.00 , Euro 5,000.00 , £ 5,000.00 dan SGD 5,000.00 , yang sedianya akan dibuat membangun Cafe. Berapakah modal yang dimiliki Andi?​


Untuk mengetahui modal yang dimiliki Andi, Anda perlu mengonversikan nilai tabungan dalam dolar AS, euro, poundsterling, dan dollar singapura ke dalam mata uang yang sama (dalam hal ini, dolar AS).

US$ 5,000.00 adalah nilai yang diketahui, jadi Anda tidak perlu mengonversi ini.

Euro 5,000.00 harus dikonversi ke dolar AS menggunakan kurs saat ini. Pada saat pengetesan, 1 Euro = 1.20 Dolar AS. Jadi, 5,000 Euro = (5,000 x 1.20) = $6,000.

£ 5,000.00 harus dikonversi ke dolar AS menggunakan kurs saat ini. Pada saat pengetesan, 1 Poundsterling = 1.35 Dolar AS. Jadi, 5,000 Poundsterling = (5,000 x 1.35) = $6,750.

SGD 5,000.00 harus dikonversi ke dolar AS menggunakan kurs saat ini. Pada saat pengetesan, 1 dollar singapura = 0.75 Dolar AS. Jadi, 5,000 dollar singapura = (5,000 x 0.75) = $3,750.

Jadi modal yang dimiliki Andi adalah :

$5,000 + $6,000 + $6,750 + $3,750 = $21,500

Andi memiliki modal sebesar $21,500 untuk membangun cafe.
Jika dirupiahkan, modal yang Andi miliki adalah Rp. 301.000.000 ($21,500 = (21,500 x 14,000))

sisanya Rp11.960.000
Diketahui : Modal sebesar Rp. 38.000.000 ingin diganti ke Dollar Singapura (SGD)
Kurs Beli : 1$ SGD = Rp. 9.300 (Harga Beli per 1$ SGD dari Pembeli)
Kurs Jual : 1$ SGD = Rp. 9.500 (Harga Jual per 1$ SGD kepada Pembeli)
Dimana untuk menukarkan modal berarti kita membagi kedalam Kurs Jual :
Rp 38.000.000 / Rp. 9.500 = didapat $4000 SGD
Dibelanjakan sebesar $2700 SGD ⇒ 4000-2700 = Sisa $1300 SGD
Kemudian ditukarkan hari ini dimana :
Kurs Beli : 1$ SGD = Rp. 9.200
Kurs Jual : 1$ SGD = Rp. 9.400
Maka kita mengkalikan kepada Kurs Beli didapat sebesar :
$1300 × Rp. 9.200 = Rp. 11.960.000,-
Sisa uang Raya hari ini sebesar Rp. 11.960.000.-

23. On a day, the exchange rate between the Singapore dollars (SGD) and the US dollars (USD) is 1 SGD = 0.816 USD, correct to 3 decimal between the places. The exchange rate Australian dollars (AUD) and the US dollars is 1 AUD = 1.073 USD, correct to 3 decimal places. (a) Find the exchange rate, correct to 3 decimal places, between Singapore dollars and Australian dollars in AUD/SGD. (b) Mr Lim has a son studying in a university in Sydney. He has to remit 25 000 AUD to him. Find the required amount of Singapore dollars correct to the nearest dollar.​


Jawaban:

(a) To find the exchange rate between Singapore dollars and Australian dollars in AUD/SGD, we can use the given exchange rates:

1 SGD = 0.816 USD

1 AUD = 1.073 USD

To convert from SGD to AUD, we can first convert SGD to USD, and then convert USD to AUD:

1 SGD = 0.816 USD

1 SGD/0.816 USD = 1.22549019608 SGD/USD (using the reciprocal of 0.816)

1 AUD = 1.073 USD

1.073 USD/1 AUD = 0.93121440823 AUD/USD (using the reciprocal of 1.073)

Now, to get the exchange rate between SGD and AUD, we can divide the AUD/USD exchange rate by the SGD/USD exchange rate:

0.93121440823 AUD/USD ÷ 1.22549019608 SGD/USD ≈ 0.759 AUD/SGD

Therefore, the exchange rate between Singapore dollars and Australian dollars in AUD/SGD is approximately 0.759, correct to 3 decimal places.

(b) To find the required amount of Singapore dollars to remit 25,000 AUD, we can use the exchange rate we found in part (a):

1 AUD = 0.759 SGD (approximately)

Therefore, 25,000 AUD is equivalent to:

25,000 AUD × 0.759 SGD/AUD ≈ 18,975 SGD

Rounding to the nearest dollar, the required amount of Singapore dollars is 18,975 SGD.
