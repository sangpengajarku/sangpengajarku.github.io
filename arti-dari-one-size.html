2. How much is one serving size?


Jawaban:

A serving size is a measured amount of food—1 cup, 1 slice, 1 teaspoon, etc. It's the amount you'll see on a food label. For example, on a box of pasta, the serving size is typically two ounces, which is about ½ cup dry or 1 cup cooked.

=960.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:
