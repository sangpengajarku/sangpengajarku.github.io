19. 20 bagian bagian tubuh dan manfaatnya dalam bahasa inggris​


1. Liver - its function is as the digestive gland secretes the bile sap which contains bile salts into the duodenum to digest fat.

2. The kidneys - are responsible for removing and regulating electrolyte balance

3. The heart - is responsible for pumping blood in the body and to give blood to the body of the organ through blood vessels.

4. Lungs - helps in breathing and is the most important organ of the respiratory system, in collaboration with the heart

5. Stomach - to get rid of stomach acid, hydrochloric acid and maintain pH levels that affect the smooth digestion

6. Small intestine - The function of the small intestine is to absorb most of the nutrients found in food, when the food is digested reaches the small intestine, it has been mechanically broken into liquid.

7. Pancreas - The pancreas is an integral part of the digestive system and the function of the head of the pancreas is to expel pancreatic fluid and insulin

8. The throat - the throat is the place for food to go into the esophagus and as a place for air or breathing to travel to the lungs

9. Nose - to breathe breathing air, filter air, warm breathing air, also play a role in sound resonance

10. Brain - to regulate and coordinate most of the body's movements, behaviors and homeostasis functions such as heart rate, blood pressure, body fluid balance and body temperature.

11. Mouth - to eat and talk. The mouth section includes the lips, vestibule, oral cavity, gums, teeth, hard and soft palate, tongue and salivary glands

12. Pineal Gland - is an important reddish gray endocrine gland that is present in the brain. The pineal gland is about the size of a rice and consists mainly of special cells called pinealocytes.

13. The hypothalamus - is part of the human brain that controls the release of the main hormone by the pituitary gland.

14. Pharynx - to filter the air. Pharynx makes the air warm, moistens and passes through this moisturizes air into the lungs

15. Larynx - to give a tone of voice when we speak. Larynx is better known as a voice box.

16. Salivary Gland - to expel saliva into the mouth to wet food. The salivary gland also starts digestion and helps in protecting teeth from decaying

17. Bile - to store bile, which is secreted by the liver until it is needed for digestion. This gallbladder also helps in fat digestion.

18. Rectum - Rectum (axis of the intestine) is a digestive organ that serves as a place to accommodate feces.

19. Diaphragm - is a partition of the muscle between the stomach and chest cavity and extends at the bottom of the rib cage. The main function of the diaphragm is to help the respiratory system.

20. Ovary - is part of the female reproductive system, which is located on the lateral wall of the pelvis and attaches to the uterus. The two ovaries are connected to each other by the fallopian tube.
