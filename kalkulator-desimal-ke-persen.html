15. QUIZ #51Tentukan pendekatan nilai (1,998)⁴ TANPA KALKULATOR DAN TANPA DIKALI MANUAL, (minimal 3 desimal)​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

(1,998)⁴

=(2-0,002)⁴

=(2-2×[tex] {10}^{ - 3} [/tex])⁴

=2⁴-4.2³.2×[tex] {10}^{ - 3} [/tex]+6.2².(2×[tex] {10}^{ - 3} [/tex])²-4.2.(2×[tex] {10}^{ - 3} [/tex])³+(2×[tex] {10}^{ - 3} [/tex])⁴

=16-64×[tex] {10}^{ - 3} [/tex]+96×[tex] {10}^{ - 6} [/tex]-64×[tex] {10}^{ - 9} [/tex]+[tex] {10}^{ - 12} [/tex]

=16-0,064+0,000096-0,000000064+0,000000000001

=15,936

Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

[tex]y = x^4 \to dy = 4\cdot x^3 \cdot dx[/tex]

1.998 => x = 2, dx = 2 - 1.998 = 0.002

maka :

[tex]dy = 4\cdot 2^3 \cdot 0.002\\\\dy = 0.064[/tex]

maka nilai nya :

[tex]1.998^4 = (x-dx)^4 \\\\(x-dx)^4 = y - dy = x^4 - dy\\\\1.998^4 = 2^4 - 0.064\\\\1.998^4 = 16 - 0.064\\\\\boxed{\boxed{\textbf{\Huge{$\boldsymbol{1.998^4 = 15.936}$}}}}[/tex]
