Named in the media as "Baadshah of Bollywood", "King of Bollywood" or "King Khan", he appeared in over 80 Bollywood films, and won numerous awards, including 14 Filmfare Awards. Khan has significant fans in Asia and Indian diaspora around the world. In terms of the number of audience and income, he is named as one of the most successful movie stars in the world.
