16. taraf intensitas 1 mesin ketik 20 db berapa taraf intensitas 10 mesin ketik


BUNYI

TI₁ = 20 dB
n = 10 → TI₁₀ = __?

TIₙ = TI₁ + 10 log n
TI₁₀ = 20 + 10 log 10
TI₁₀ = 20 + 10
TI₁₀ = 30 dB ✔️1 mesin tik =20 db
berapa db jika 10 mesin tik?
1=20
10=10*20=200

I = I₀ 10^(TI / 10)
  = (10⁻¹² W/m²) 10^(70 dB / 10) = 10⁻⁵ W/m²
