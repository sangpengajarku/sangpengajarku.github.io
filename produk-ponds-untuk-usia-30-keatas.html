16. The backyard of a house is rectangular with a length of 10 m and a width of 6 m. 16 part is used for ponds, 34part is planted with grass, and the rest is covered with coral rock. What is the area of backyard covered with coral rock? Bantu plssSSSS


Jawab:

1/12

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

1/6 & 3/4 = 2/12 & 9/12

2/12 + 9/12 = 11/12

12/12 - 11/12 = 1/12

1/12 x 60 = 60 ÷ 12

=5cm²

So, the area of backyard covered with the coral rock is 5cm². :)
