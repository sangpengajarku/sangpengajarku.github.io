18. Kuis: super mudah Jika f(x) = x²-4, dan g(x) = x²-9 Maka nilai daripada (f-g)(x) = .....


➩ Diketahui :

f(x) = x² - 4

g(x) = x² - 9

➩ Ditanya :

(f-g) (x)?

➩ Jawab :

[tex]\sf \bf \tt (f-g)(x) [/tex]

[tex]\sf \bf \tt = f(x) - g(x) [/tex]

[tex]\sf \bf \tt = (x² - 4) - ( x² - 9)[/tex]

[tex]\sf \bf \tt = (x² - 4) - x² + 9[/tex]

[tex]\sf \bf \tt = x² - x² - 4 + 9[/tex]

[tex]\sf \bf \tt = 0 - 4 + 9[/tex]

[tex]\sf \bf \tt = - 4 + 9[/tex]

[tex]\sf \bf \tt = 9-4[/tex]

[tex]\bold{\underline{\boxed{\green{\sf \bf \tt = 5}}}}[/tex]

25. Perbadaan imun spesifikasi dan non spesifikasi
