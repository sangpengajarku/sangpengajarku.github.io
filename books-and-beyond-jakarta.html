9. What is the difference between paperback books and hardback books ?


Jawaban:

Paperback books, also known as softcover books are books with its cover made with a thick stock layer or a paperboard. It's binding between pages uses glue. Since it's thin, you'll need to take care of it to not let it tear. Luckily, the price of paperback books are cheaper than the harback ones. Good if you want to save money!

Hardcover books are books with a hard, heavy protective cover, just like its name. Made from a thick cardboard wrapped in a dust-free material. Although the price is more expensive, it offers more protection to the book.
