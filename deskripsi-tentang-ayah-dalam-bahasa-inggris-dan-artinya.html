8. contoh deskripsi bahasa inggris tentang ayah


Here's the example of describing a father figure.

A father is one irreplaceable role in a family. It is necessary as well as crucial for a normal functioning family. My father is one respected example of it. He is a man of principle who always shows the importance of ethics and respects in his life. He has become a role model for his childern. Physically, he does not have an ideal figure of a man nor is considered attractive either. He is only average-height, 170 cm tall to be exact and average-weight as well. He has short wavy hair and thin lips. My father might be considered as smart, talkative and tactful. He always attempts to utter diplomatically anytime anywhere. Although he can be persistent and stubborn, he is willing to learn something new, eventhough it takes time. In his spare time, my old man is into painting, watching movies and reading. His favourite fields of reading are history, philosophy, art and islamic studies apart from his major of study back then, electrical engineering. He is also a practical man as well. No wonder that he often fixes any broken stuffs around the house on weekends.

Penjelasan:

In writing descriptive text, one has to try to give a detailed description on the subject one wants to describe. That is as a matter of fact, the main purpose of such kind of text. In terms of describing a person, like a father, the details include

The physical appearance The personalityThe general opinion about the subjects.The interestThe routine

Those five elements can be found in the example above. Here is the details

Physical appearrance. It's stated in the sentence 'Physically, he does .... thin lips.'Personality. It's uttered in the sentence 'My father might be.....it takes time.'The general opinion about the subject, which can be seen in the sentence 'My father might be considered as...tactful.'The interest. It can be seen in the sentence 'In his spare time, ......electrical engineering.'The routine. It can be seen in the sentence 'He often.....the house.'

21. tuliskan deskripsi tentang ayah dalam bahasa inggris


PART : DESCRIBING PEOPLE

------------------------------------

Jawaban:

I have a lovely family. My father is a brave man in our family. He works hard for our family. My father is an architect. His duty is designing the building concept. He has been working for almost twenty years. ( Identification )

My father is a hard worker. He works consistently and responsibly.  Besides his duty as an architect, he never neglects his work at house. He is not a lazy man. He always does everything by himself , such as washing his car, watering plants in the garden and accompanying me in finishing the school tasks. His hobby is fishing. He goes fishing every Sunday with my brother ( description of behavior)

He is a handsome man. He has a dimple on each checks. He is tall , slim and strapping. He is a nice person . He always helps others. That's the reason, both neighbors and his  friends like him. ( description of appearance )

That is my father's description. We love him so much. He is good figure of a father in my family.  I'm proud of him. ( conclusion )

TRANSLATION :

Aku memiliki keluarga yang menyenangkan. Ayah ku  adalah pria pemberani di keluarga kami. Dia bekerja keras untuk keluarga kami.

Ayah saya adalah seorang arsitek. Tugasnya adalah merancang konsep bangunan. Dia telah bekerja selama hampir dua puluh tahun. Dia pekerja keras. Dia bekerja secara konsisten dan bertanggung jawab. Selain tugasnya sebagai arsitek, dia tidak pernah mengabaikan pekerjaannya di rumah. Dia bukan pria yang malas. Dia selalu melakukan segalanya sendiri, seperti mencuci mobil, menyiram tanaman di kebun dan menemaniku menyelesaikan tugas sekolah.

Dia pria yang tampan. Dia memiliki lesung pipi di setiap pipinya. Dia tinggi, langsing dan tegap. Dia orang yang baik. Dia sukan membantu sesama.  Itulah alasannya, baik tetangga maupun teman-temannya menyukainya. Hobinya adalah memancing. Dia pergi memancing setiap minggu dengan kakakku.

Itu deskripsi ayah ku. Kami sangat mencintainya. Dia sosok ayah yang baik di keluarga. Aku bangga terhadap nya .

Penjelasan:

DESCRIPTIVE TEXT

Genre teks yang menjabarkan suatu benda/orang/tempat dan hal lainnya secara spesifik.

Social function/ communicative purpose :

to describe people/place/things specifically ( untuk mendeskripsikan orang/tempat/hal -hal secara khusus )

Generic Structure

~ Identification

Bagian paragraf ini  terletak di awal sebagai pengenalan siapa/apa yang dijabarkan.

~Description

Bagian paragraf selanjutnya menjabarkan ciri khas dari orang/benda/tempat/hal yang di bicarakan.

~ Conclusion

Akhir penutup paragraf  berisi kesimpulan dari si penulis. Bagian ini optional ( Bisa dibahas atau pun tidak )
