Bango or Bango soy sauce is a sweet soy sauce brand produced by PT. Anugrah Setia Lestari for PT. Unilever Indonesia Tbk. Kecap Bango often makes Bango Snack Festival events to introduce authentic Indonesian culinary. History: Bango soy sauce was originally a home industry that began in 1928 in Benteng, Tangerang, West Java. Bango's journey was started by Tjoa Pit Boen who was first peddled in a small shop in his garage. The name Bango was chosen by its founder with one vision, namely that its products can fly high to foreign countries.

When Yunus Kartadinata's business expanded, the Kecap Bango factory moved on Jalan Asem Lama (now Jalan Wahid Hasyim), Tanah Abang, Central Jakarta, just behind the Election Supervisory Body building. But the area has been turned into a row of office houses. Bango soy sauce is growing and popular in West Java and Jakarta.

The Kecap Bango company turned into a limited liability company, namely PT Anugrah Indah Pelangi and PT Anugrah Damai Pratama. Management was managed by Yunus' fourth child, Eppy Kartadinata, in 1982. The factory now occupies an area of ​​eight hectares in Wantilan Village, Cipeundeuy, Subang, West Java.

22. 3x²+13x-10:0 Mengunakan cara kecap atau abc


HP {⅔ , -5}

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

a = 3

b = 13

c = -10

x1,2 = (-b ± √(b² - 4ac)) / 2a

= (-13 ± √(13² - 4(3)(-10))) / 2(3)

= (-13 ± √(169 + 120)) / 6

= (-13 ± √289) / 6

= (-13 ± 17) / 6

x1 = (-13 + 17) / 6

= 4/6

= ⅔

x2 = (-13 - 17) / 6

= -30/6

= -5
