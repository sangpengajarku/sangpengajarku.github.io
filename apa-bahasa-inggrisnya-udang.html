24. sebutkan ciri-ciri udang dalam bahasa inggris!


a.Struktur Body

Shrimp segmented body (segmented) and consists of sefalotoraks (head and chest into one) and the abdomen (stomach). Part anterior (front) and a large body is wider, while the posterior (rear end) it narrow.
At the head of the mouth there are some tools, namely:
1) 2 pairs of antennae
2) 1 pair of mandibles to bite its prey
3) 1 pair of maxillary
4) 1 pair maksilliped
Maxilla and maksiliped serves to filter out the food and deliver food to the mouth. Locomotor form of legs (one pair each segment of the abdomen), and serves to swim, crawl or 'stick' in the bottom waters.
