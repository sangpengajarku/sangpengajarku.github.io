prosen volume metanol dan etanol ?
jawab :
volume total = (250 + 50 + 25 )ml = 325 ml
prosen volume metanol
= 50ml/325 ml x 100% = 5000/325 = 15,38%
prosen volume etanol
= 25 ml/325 ml x 100% = 2500/325 = 7,69%
