Lose /luz/ adalah irregular verb (kata kerja tidak beraturan) yang bermakna “fail to win” (gagal menang), “be unable to find” (tidak dapat menemukan), atau “fail to keep” (gagal menjaga/mempertahankan) yang membicarakan aksi; sementara loose /lus/ adalah adjective yang berarti “not tight fitting” (tidak ketat), “not fixed” (tidak tetap, tetap, atau kaku), “not fastened” (tidak diikat) atau “relaxed” (santai) untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu. 16. Cerita tentang diri sendiri dalam bahasa Inggris yanh di dalamnya ada !!! Simple present tenar Simple past tense Simple future tense Minimal 300 kata


Writing using Simple Past Tense, Simple Present Tense and Simple Future Tense

This is a story about me. My name is Aria Suryatama. I am 15 years old. I live in Jakarta which is the capital city of Indonesia. Actually, I come from Semarang, but two years ago, my family moved to Jakarta. I have a big sister, Arita, she is 20 years old and she studies engineering in University of Indonesia. She is going to graduate next year. My father is a scientist and my mother is a high school teacher.

I am interested in computer science, music and sport. When I was 13 years old, I had got my first PC as a birthday present, at first I only played computer games but then I notice I could do a lot more things with the computer, for example programming. I hope that in the future I will get a job as a computer scientist.

In music, I play piano and guitar. I join a band at school. We practice twice a week after school. It is a lot of fun playing music instruments. In our spare time, I and my family usually play music, singing or play board games at home.

My dream is to study computer science and to go to South Korea. I always love everything associated with this country. I hope that I can visit South Korea in the future and stay there for many years. With all I already have, I know that I’m on the right path and I will do my best to inspire others.

Pembahasan

Berikut pembahasan mengenai tenses yang digunakan dalam cerita diatas:

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE

Simple Present Tense adalah tenses dalam bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk menyatakan habit (kebiasaan), unchanging situation, general truth dan fixed arrangement.

Bagaimana membentuk simple present tense?

Simple present tense memiliki beberapa rule:

Jika subyeknya I, we, they maka menggunakan verb-1 seperti like, want, need, love. Sedangkan bentuk negatifnya menggunakan DO+NOT.Jika subyeknya orang ketiga tunggal seperti he, she, my sister, Ana, Tommy maka menggunakan verb-1+s/es seperti likes, wants, needs, loves. Bentuk negatifnya menggunakan DOES+NOT.Keterangan waktu yang biasanya mengikuti simple present tense diantaranya always, sometimes, every day, every saturday, once a week, usually, normally dan lain sebagainya.

Contoh kalimat yang menggunakan simple present tense dalam cerita diatas adalah:

I am 15 years old.I live in Jakarta.She studies engineering in University of Indonesia.My father is a scientist.We practice twice a week after school.SIMPLE PAST TENSE

Simple past tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk kegiatan yang sudah diselesaikan pada waktu lampau. Kejadian tersebut bisa saja baru saja terjadi dan sudah selesai atau pada beberapa waktu yang lalu.

Bentuk dari Simple Past Tense

(+) Subject + Verb-2

(-) Subject + did not + infinitive without to

(?) Did + subject + infinitive without to

Contoh kalimat yang menggunakan simple past tense dalam cerita diatas adalah:

My family moved to Jakarta.At first, I only played computer games.

SIMPLE FUTURE TENSE

Simple Future Tense adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk membicarakan masa depan (future). Pola yang digunakan dalam simple future tense adalah:

Subject + Will + infinitive without to

contoh kalimat yang menggunakan simple future tense dalam cerita diatas:

I hope in the future, I will get a job as a computer scientist.I will do my best to inspire others.Pelajari Lebih Lanjut

20. Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous


Recount Text

Recount Text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menceritakan pengalaman atau kejadian yang dialami di masa lampau.  

Generic Structure dari Recount text adalah:

1. Orientation: Berisi pengenalan tokoh, tempat, waktu dan tujuan cerita.

2. Events: Berisi hal-hal yang terjadi pada waktu itu (lampau).

3. Re-orientation: Berisi kesimpulan cerita atau kesan dari penulis/ tokoh terhadap pengalaman/ ceritanya.

Language features dari Recount Text:

1. Menggunakan Past tense

2. Menggunakan action verb

3. Menggunakan thinking verb.

Pembahasan

Cerita bahasa inggris dengan kata kerja simple present, simple past, present continous adalah:

This is my experience in middle school, exactly 3 years ago when I was in 7th grade. Every year, our school held scout activities which must be followed by all 7th grade students and at the end of each semester there were camp activities held at the school.

When the D-day arrived, before the activity began the opening ceremony was held to get information and direction during the camp activities. After that, my team mate and I immediately set up a tent that was assisted by a coach. After the tent was established, we immediately arranged the equipment we had brought. Shortly, the whistle rang, I quickly put on a tie and other scout gear because there would be a game between teams to train the cohesiveness and togetherness of each team. Then there was heavy rain. Considering that all the equipment was in the tent, the school finally decided that all students would take refuge in the hall, let the conditions where it was not possible to come in because there were several tents which had collapsed by the wind, and the field were very muddy. Arriving at the hall, we immediately rushed to the bathroom to change our clothes.  

After each team gathered according to each class, everyone got a box of rice which could be enjoyed together. After dinner, the rain stopped. We went straight to the courtyard to enjoy the campfire and watch the performances of each team, in the form of songs and poems. After the campfire event was over, the school announced that the female students in that night must sleep in the meeting room, while the men remained in the hall. Finally we were moved again. In our hearts, we were very happy because of the meeting room has an air conditioner, and it might not be fair for men to have to sleep without air conditioner.

The next morning at around 5 a.m, all students had to get up and get ready, because morning exercise would be held. After that, we enjoyed the breakfast together. We didn't forget to clear the tent that had collapsed and saved it again. Around 8 o'clock, we prepared for the closing ceremony. This was an unforgettable experience.

30. contoh kalimat aktif dan pasif menggunakan kata kerja makan simple present tense dalam bahasa inggris​


Kata kerja makan (eat)

Kalimat Aktif: You eat fried rice every morning Kalimat Pasif: Fried rice is eaten by you every morning

PEMBAHASANActive-Passive VoiceActive Voice adalah kalimat aktif yang berisi Subject melakukan sebuah aksi atau tindakan dalam kalimatnya.Passive Voice adalah kalimat Pasif yang berisi Object kalimat yang diberi aksi atau diberi tindakan oleh Subject.Pola dasar Passive Voice adalah Be+V3.

Sebelumnya mari kita bahas apa itu Present Tense?

➡️ Present Tense Yang digunakan untuk menggambarkan Kegiatan berulang dan menyatakan kebenaran umum. Keterangan waktu yang menunjukkan waktu present tense:

Every dayEvery morningRegularlyAlwaysusuallyoften

Setelah kita tahu bahwa Kalimat soal adalah Present Tense karena ada ket. waktu everyday. mari kita lanjutkan ke rumusnya:

Active-Present Tense

(+) S + V1 (-s: es) + O

➡ You eat fried rice every morning

Subject => You (kamu)Verb-1 => eat (makan)Object => fried rice (nasi goreng) Adverb of time => Every morning (setiap pagi)

Keterangan:

Subjek dalam kalimat adalah You (kamu) dan melakukan aksi yaitu makan (eat)

dan kita berlanjut ke perubahan selanjutnya.

Passive-Present Tense

(+) O + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By S

➡ Fried rice is eaten by you every morning

Object => fried rice (nasi goreng)Be + Verb-3 => is + eaten (dimakan)By Subject => By you (oleh Kamu)Adverb of time => Every morning (setiap pagi

Keterangan:

Object, nasi goreng (Fried rice) diberi aksi yaitu dimakan (is eaten) oleh Subjek (by You)
