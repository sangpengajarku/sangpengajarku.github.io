perbandingan yang Kate dan Norra = 3 : 5
misal uang Kate = 3x dan uang Nora = 5x
(3x+150 ) : (5x-150) = 7 : 9
9(3x+150) = 7(5x-150)
27x  + 1.350 = 35x - 1.050
27x - 35x = -1050 + 1.350
- 8 x = - 300
x = 37,5
uang Kate = 3x = 3(37,5) = $ 112,5
uang Nora = 5x = 5(37,5) = $ 187,5
