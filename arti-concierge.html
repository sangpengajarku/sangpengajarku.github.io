25. This conversation is between a concierge at a hotel and a costumer . A: good evening . Can i help you? B: good evening. Yes, please. I would like a room for the night . A: ....... B: ...... A:..... B: thanks . Good Night . Lanjutkan yg kosong


Arti dari "This conversation is between a concierge at a hotel and a costumer" adalah ini adalah percakapan antara pramutamu di sebuah hotel dengan seorang tamu. A concierge adalah pramutamu atau istilah lainnya adalah receiptionist atau costumer service.

Pembahasan

At the hotel (dialog 1)

A: Good evening. Can I help you? (offering help)

B: Good evening. Yes please. I would like a room for the night.

A: Would you like a single room, or a double room? (offering something)

B: A single room, please. But I came with my friend. Is the room too small for my friend and I?

A: I think you should take a double room, Sir. (suggesting something)

B : Thanks. Good night.

Dialog 2

A: Good evening. Can I help you? (offering help)

B: Good evening. Yes please. I would like a room for the night.

A: Would you like a single room, or a double room? (offering something)

B: A single room, please.

A: Would you like smoking or non smoking room, Sir? (suggesting something)

B: Non-smoking, please. Thanks. Good night.

Guest        : "Thank you"

Concierge : "How about your trip, sir/madam?"

Concierge : "I suggest you to visit Asia Afrika Street. It is not too far, just take 30 minutes by car"
Guest        : "Yes, I think so. I'll go there tomorrow. Could you tell me the facilities of your hotel?"
Concierge : "Of course, sir/madam. We have a swimming pool at back of the hotel, bar and lounge that open until the midnight, spa and sauna, fitness center, restaurant and coffeeshop. You can get a free wi-fi too.
Guest        : "Thanks for your information"
Concierge : "Yes, sir/madam"


Concierge : "May I explain your room facilities, sir/madam?"
Guest        : "Yes, Please"

Follow me to another room, the television with more than 50 channels such as national and international channel. And this is for control your Air Condition, turn left for minimum cool and turn right for maximum cool. Now, we're going to the bathroom. If you turn it left, that's for shower, and right for bath tub. You can control the water too by combine the cold one with the hot one for make a warm water.
