Laju Pekerjaan

1 / t = (1 / 21) + (1 / 35)
1 / t = (1 × 5 / 21 × 5) + (1 × 3 / 35 × 3)
1 / t = 5 / 105 + 3 / 105
1 / t = 5 + 3 / 105
1 / t = 8 / 105
t = 105 / 8
